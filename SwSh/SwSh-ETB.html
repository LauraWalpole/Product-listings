Contents

Each Elite Trainer Box contains:

  • 8 Pokémon TCG: Sword & Shield: [expansion] booster packs
  • 65 card sleeves featuring [Pokemon]
  • 45 Pokémon TCG Energy cards
  • A player’s guide to the Sword & Shield: [expansion] expansion
  • A Pokémon TCG rulebook
  • 6 damage-counter dice
  • 1 competition-legal coin-flip die
  • 2 acrylic condition markers
  • A collector’s box to hold everything, with 4 dividers to keep it organized
  • A code card for the Pokémon Trading Card Game Online
